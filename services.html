Our Services

Our Dog Care Professionals are happy to provide the following services.

Our Mission is to provide your dog with unconditional love they so urgently need when you are away. Your dog will feel loved, happy,and sage in a home enviroment with ceramic flooring.

Other features include:

Walls instead of chain link, climate control ensure clean air for health, comforting music 24/7, fleece blankets, kong treats, playground toys, cots and balls for stimulation and fun.

*We don't walk more than 3 dogs at a time.

Every dog needs at least 30 minutes of exercise each day. Let us walk your four legged friend and let them enjoy the outdoors!

Our professional walkers teach your dog how to walk on a leash. Since every dog has different personalities and behaviors, we treat each dog different based on those needs.

First walk on us!!
*Applies to new customers only

(Includes dogs and cats only)

When you are away let us visit your cat or dog to make sure they have all the things they need! Food, water, and all the unconditional love to help them feel safe and happy unil your return.

Happy Tail LLC understands the need for pet health and safety, that is why we offer such services such as performing at home shots, injections, and/or pill administrations per your Veterinarian's request.

Please note that while we are able to perform these administrations, Happy Tail LLC is not veterinary office. Please ensure that your lovely pet has been kept up to date on all of their shots, vaccines, and yearly checkups before booking!

This and the above services always come with extra special care. Please note: We want your pet to feel safe with us, so we like to have an introduction interview with you as the owner and your dog before actually caring for him or her. Please call us soon!

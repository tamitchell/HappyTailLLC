Meet the Founder

Fiorella Diaz Gambini

I am a young entrepreneur as I do what I love the most taking care of dogs and cats.

I have my own dog, he is a three years old Great Pyrenees and I am so grateful everyday for having him in my life.

Since I was a little girl my biggest love in life was caring for animals. I love and understand each one with their different personalities (dogs and cats are my favorites of course!)

To me, Happy Tail LLC is not just a company, it is my passion and dream come true. I love and care for others. Throughout my life I have learned so much thanks to all the pets I have been around. I started company during July of 2015, but I have been working with animals since my childhood. Back then, I would ask my neighbors to walk their dogs or pet sit their cats.

I am happy to have started this business. By doing what I love, I can keep meeting wonderful dogs and cats!

We believe there are three steps to establishing a good business. Those are:

A Reliable Team

Insured, licensed, and bonded. We perform background checks on every employee.

A Peaceful Mission

Our mission is to provide peace of mind to our client and assure the best care for you and your best friend

A dedicated Vision

We carry out the vision of a happy environment where your four-legged friend will live to the fullest in a safe environment.
